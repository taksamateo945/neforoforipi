    Little Justice Jay looks just like her mama! Danneel Harris gave the first glimpse of her newborn daughter with husband Jensen Ackles while going through security at Los Angeles International Airport on Tuesday, July 23. Looking radiant without makeup, the new mom cradled her mini-me, who wore a pink and white polka dot onesie.

    The former One Tree Hill actress, 34, and the Supernatural star, 35, announced their daughter's birth via Harris' Twitter account in early June. Calling their firstborn "precious," Harris said their little girl "was born happy and healthy on May 30." The Louisiana native announced her pregnancy in January, just before the People's Choice Awards.

    Harris has been relatively quiet on Twitter since Justice Jay's arrival, though her husband opened up to E! News about being a first-time parent during Comic-Con in San Diego, Calif. "It's been quite the adjustment," said the star, who wed Harris in his hometown of Houston, Texas, in 2010. "A friend of mine says it's a slightly different gear, and I said, 'Different gear? No, you put it in park, you get out of the car, you get in a different car, you take a left down a wrong street. That's basically where I'm at now."
