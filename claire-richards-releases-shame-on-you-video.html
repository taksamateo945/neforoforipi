    Claire Richards has kicked off her 2019 with the release of Shame On You, the third single taken from her upcoming album My Wildest Dreams.

    Shame On You is an anthemic, pure pop track with Claire’s powerful vocals which make you get up on your feet and to the nearest dancefloor. Teaming up with Cutfather (Kylie, Little Mix), Chelcee Grimes (Dua Lipa, Olly Murs) and Karen Harding this explains why we can’t stop ourselves singing along.

    Speaking of the track, Claire explains “Shame On You is the most upbeat, fun song on my album and I am super excited for everyone to hear it. It’s an empowering anthem for anyone who’s ever been treated badly in a relationship and documents the moment they realise they deserve so much more. It got everyone on their feet on my recent My Wildest Dreams tour and it’s quickly become a fan favourite.”

    Shame On You is taken from Claire’s debut solo album My Wildest Dreams set for release on February 1st through Sony Music. It follows the previously singles On My Own and End Before We Start, showing her empowering lyric ability throughout all tracks we can’t wait to see what the album holds! Last month saw her embark on a sold-out tour which saw her finishing off at London’s Union Chapel, read all about the show here. We have a feeling there’s going to be many more opportunities to see Claire in 2019 and we can’t wait.
