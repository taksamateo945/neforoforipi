    American football cornerback, Patrick Peterson plays for NFL team, Arizona Cardinals. The player has a long association of almost eight years with Cardinals after playing two years for his college team.

    Patrick Peterson Bio & Family

    The player was born as Petrick De’mon Peterson Jr. on 11 July 1990 in Pompano Beach, Florida to parents, Patrick Peterson Sr. and Shandra Paterson. He went to Blanche Ely High School, and his junior playing stats were 11 total touchdowns, seven interceptions and 54 tackles on defense. Similarly, in his senior year, his stats were 733 yards rush and 11 touchdowns where his defensive side was 21 tackles and five interceptions.

    His other family member includes of his younger brother, Avery Johnson. The Cornerback stands at the height of 6 feet 1 inch (1.85 m) and weighs around 92 kg.

    Career, Contract & Net Worth

    The 28-year-old played two years for his college, Louisiana State University’s team, LSU Tigers football team from 2008 to 2010. In his freshman season, his stats were 41 tackles, one interception, and three pass breakups including a forced fumble and a quarterback hurry. The next season followed his sophomore year where he had a 37-yard interception return for a touchdown.

    In the year 2009, he finished the season with 52 tackles and was also won Second-Team All-American by The Sporting News. Similarly on 27 November 2010, his impressive stats were four kick returns for 163 net yards, three total tackles, and one pass defense. Well, the player got the fortune to play with NFL team, the Cardinals after signing a guaranteed four-year contract worth $18.42 million on 30 July 2011. Apart of his new signing contract also included a signing bonus of $11.90 million.

    Likewise, three years later, he agreed upon a five-year contract worth $70 million on 29 July 2014. The agreement also included a signing bonus of $48 million guarantees and a $15.3 million signing bonus. Apart from the multi-million dollar deal, Patrick does not have any injury as if for now.
