    She’s Bootylicious, Flawless, and a certified Diva, and now Beyonce’s the perfect meme! The new hashtag “#BeyonceAlwaysOnBeat” started trending over Memorial Day weekend and featured the Grammy winner, 33, dancing in music videos and live performances. Instead of pop, lock, and dropping to her own tunes, other songs have been swapped in to show that Beyonce is, well, always on beat! 

    Most of the added music doesn’t match Beyonce’s genre or style, ranging from ‘80s rock hits to gospel tunes, making the memes particularly hilarious.

    Sadly Queen Bey hasn’t responded to the meme with a clip of her own, but while we wait, there’s plenty of Vines, Instagram videos, and clips available for our viewing pleasure.
