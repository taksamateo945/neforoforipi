    Tyler Gregory Okonma, better known as Tyler the Creator, is an American rapper and record producer recognized for his clever rap rhymes. Despite receiving controversy for his previous albums for using homophobic and sexist obscenities in his songs, the musician has had a huge effect on children born in the 2000s. He rose to prominence as a co-founder of the alternative hip hop group Odd Future.

    He made a reputation for himself in the music business with his album ‘Flower Boy,’ which earned him his second Grammy nomination for Best Rap Album at the 60th Annual Grammy Awards. At the 62nd Grammy Awards, another of his albums, ‘Igor,’ received the prize for Best Rap Album. The artist is also known for his signature tattoos, which he posts on Instagram, as he has the name of each record inked on his body. In this piece, we’ll look at Tyler’s numerous inks.

    Tattoo of Tyler the Creator’s Guardian Angel Inhaler

    Artist Matt McCormick tattooed an inhaler with wings on Tyler’s left thigh. Tyler links inhaler ink with strength because he routinely carries the device to his gigs. “I’ll be sorry when I no longer have baby lungs,” he stated of his ‘Guardian Angel Inhaler’ tattoo.

    Thigh Tattoos Of Tyler the Creator

    Tyler has “GOLF WANG” tattooed on both thighs, a reference to the clothing company he launched in 2011. The word was derived from Tyler’s early use of the slang term Wolf Gang. His right upper leg has a little dog tattoo. On his right thigh, he has the N.E.R.D. ‘Brain’ logo tattooed. Pharrell Williams leads the rock and hip hop band N.E.R.D. Tyler’s life was greatly influenced by the band, which was established in 1999 in Virginia Beach, Virginia.

    His Albums inspire Tyler the Creator’s Tattoos.

    The rapper has also had tattoos on his thighs that recall the titles of his albums. The words BASTARD, GOBLIN, and WOLF appear over his thighs. His most popular albums were released in 2009, 2011, and 2013, consecutively. Later, he added three more names to the list with subsequent releases: CHERRY BOMB, FLOWER BOY, and I.G.O.R., released in 2015, 2017, and 2019, respectively. Tyler, the inventor, has the word ‘RADICAL’ tattooed on his right inner arm.
