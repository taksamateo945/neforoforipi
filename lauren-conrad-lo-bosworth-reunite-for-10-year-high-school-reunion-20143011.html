    26 May 2024, 00:00

    Trip down memory lane! BFFs Lauren Conrad and Lo Bosworth reunited with fellow Laguna Beach castmates at their 10-year high school reunion on Saturday, Nov. 29, in Laguna Beach, Calif.

    “Um, we invented Post-Its. #LBHS10YearReunion @laurenconrad” Bosworth captioned a selfie with her bestie at the reunion, quoting 1997 comedy Romy and Michele's High School Reunion.

    Fellow MTV alum Jen Bunney also took a trip back to the high school where all their drama played out on TV with her new husband Taylor Dunphy. “10 yr laguna beach high school reunion,” she captioned a picture of herself looking all grown up in a chic black dress.

    LC’s ex-crush Stephen Colletti also attended the event. The boys of the hit reality show did reunite this past September though, at friend Ben Singer’s wedding in the beach town.

    In an adorable Instagram, cast member Dieter Schmitz posted a group shot with Colletti and Trey Phillips alongside a throwback picture from prom ten years ago with the caption: "From senior prom to 10 years later for the first wedding last weekend for Ben @benjammin2686 @jonathonbernard @stephencolletti @treyphillips lucky to have such great friends my whole life."
