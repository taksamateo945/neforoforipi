    Social media can’t get over Justin Trudeau’s new look! The Canadian Prime Minister now rocks a beard and everyone has an opinion on it!

    It seems like a lot of people think that he looks like a total ‘babe’ like that, while others disagree, saying he looked more ‘clueless’ than anything else!

    The politician, who is generally regarded as an attractive man, debuted his new facial hair while at a number of different news conferences in the New Year.

    The change was a huge difference from the look he had only days earlier in a clip he posted online to wish his followers a Happy Hanukkah.

    At the time, he was still clean shaven, which is how people usually see him in public.

    But then the beard appeared, seemingly out of nowhere, and a lot of his fans gushed over his new face, labeling him a ‘babe.’

    One user even joked that the beard might actually help the country!

    ‘Found out today that PM Justin Trudeau has a beard now, uniting the country with the absolute fact that he has a nice beard,’ they tweeted.

    Unfortunately, however, it turns out that this particular user could not be farther from the truth as the country was actually pretty split down the middle over the look change.
