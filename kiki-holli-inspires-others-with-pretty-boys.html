    You know those confident dance-pop anthems that just leave you feeling ready to become your best self? Songs like Dua Lipa’s “Watch Me Dance” and Meghan Trainor’s “Mother” do just that. Another song that is the ideal add to your feel-good playlist is KiKi Holli’s “PRETTY BOYS”. With rising synths and effervescent horns, this infectious tune will skyrocket you into full self-assurance. The LGBTQ+ community has historically fought for equality. Though we have come a long way as a society, that community still deals with judgment to this day. That injustice has resulted in a group that shines with immense strength and conviction. KiKi Holli fearlessly expresses that fiery passion in her release. Mixing vintage instrumentation with modern flair, it is easy to get hooked into this enthralling offering.

    The Los Angeles-based indie pop artist possesses a unique style of bold sonics that burst with rich color. Her intimate lyrics and soulful tone create music that is wholly mesmerizing. Focusing on themes of resurgence, self-expression and staying present, her tracks truly hit home for her fans. From a young age KiKi Holli was always drawn to music and theater. That is heard in the single’s fierce, theatrical air.
