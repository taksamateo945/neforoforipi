    11 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Phoenix Melody is soon to be the name on everyone’s lips. After first impacting on the music world as a contestant on Australia’s X Factor in 2013, the Sydney based vocalist is now ready to turn the industry on its head with her refreshing slice of RnB pop.

    ‘Be The One’ is Phoenix’s debut offering, and it’s a certified pop banger. With production from multi-platinum hit makers DBF Production and Tha Hitbangaz, the song merges afrobeats rhythms, deep bass stabs and razor sharp RnB vocals from Phoenix. The chorus, in particular, is incredibly infectious and we guarantee that you’ll be bopping along to ‘Be The One’ from the off.

    As debut tracks go, ‘Be The One’ firmly places Phoenix as one to watch. With the summer months ahead of us, we’re certain that you’ll be hearing a lot of this song in clubs and festivals around the world.

    Why not take a listen for yourself? We have the exclusive premiere of ‘Be The One’ on CelebMix today, so hit play and get ready to bask in Phoenix’s glorious dance vibes.

    As we mentioned earlier, Phoenix first rose to fame as a contestant on Australia’s X Factor. After going all the way to the semi finals, teary-eyed celebrations included, she decided to take a small step away from writing and performing.

    However, in a recent interview with Cliché mag, Phoenix spoke about how she always remained positive about her musical future. “My music really reflects my personality. I’m laid-back, but also super outgoing,” she said. “Overall, I’m all about finding that balance and enforcing positivity – Knowing yourself allows more room for positive thinking and less room for negative energy.”

    ‘Be The One’ is just the beginning for Phoenix Melody. With a new found hope and positivity in her music, we can expect her first full EP later this year. There’ll also be a remix package for ‘Be The One’, as well as an official music video.

    We’re tipping Phoenix Melody to become a big name this year – don’t say we didn’t warn you! Keep up to date with her on Instagram,  Facebook, Twitter and through her website.

    ‘Be The One’ is available now on Spotify and iTunes.
