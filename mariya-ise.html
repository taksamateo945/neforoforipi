    Mariya Ise Biography

    Ise Mariya was born in Yokohama, Kanagawa, Japan on September 25, 1988. Ise was exposed to anime in 1997, at the age of nine, after watching Princess Mononoke in the theater and being fascinated by it. Following that, she became interested in working in the anime business.

    Mariya Ise is 5 feet 1 inch tall. She weighs approximately 50 kg. She has lovely warm black eyes and Black locks. There is no information on her chest-waist-hip measurements, dress size, shoe size, biceps, and so on.

    Career

    • Ise attended the shooting of a musical cartoon for HUNTER HUNTER later in life. She chatted with the workers while she was there. She made the decision to become a seiyuu at that time. She was exposed to a seiyuu coaching school and grew even more fascinated after learning about the many occupations a seiyuu may do. She persuaded her parents to allow her to attend the coaching school, which she paid for with money she had saved since elementary school.
    • In 2004, she made her voice acting debut in Aishiteruze Baby, playing a small part. In 2007, she landed her first major role as Kasugano Urara in Yes! PreCure 5. Her jobs continued to pile up from then, and she has now established herself as a successful seiyuu.
    • She switched agencies in 2009, from Bring-Up to Across Entertainment.
    • She married in 2015 and gave birth to her kid the following year.
    • She announced her divorce on August 12, 2022, using her official Twitter account.
    • She was a part of the seiyuu idol group PNGN6 (2008-only? ), which was inspired by the anime Penguin GirlHeart (2008).

    Mariya Ise’s Net Worth in 2023

    Mariya’s net worth is believed to be between $1 million – $5 million as of December 2023. Her excellent profession as a voice actress is obviously her primary source of money.

    Mariya Ises Husband, Marriage

    She relocated her workplace from Bring-up to Across Entertainment on October 1, 2009. She married in January of this year. She announced on her blog in the autumn of that year that she had given birth to her first kid. In August 2022, she divorced and gained exclusive custody of her kid.
